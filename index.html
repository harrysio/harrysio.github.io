“Witness my life”, Installation with Digital Video, fishnet, whiteboard on the floor, 2018

Exploration of the life of fishes, and letting the viewers experience the process as one of the fishes. As I project the video on the floor, the viewer may stand around the fishnet. The video includes four parts: catching the fishes, selling the fishes, cooking the fishes, and eating the fishes.
